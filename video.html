Bioteknologi Keju





Ingredients:

  • 2 glass of fresh milk
  • 4 tsp of vinegar
  • Salt


Instructions:

  • Prepare all the ingredients and equipment. Ensure all necessary items have been gathered.
  • Pour the milk inside a pan and heat it up. The milk should be heated in a pan until it boils or reaches around 90 degrees Celsius while stirring.
  • After boiling, the milk boiled water should be cooled to a temperature of 30 degrees Celsius.
  • Add the vinegar and stir for a few minutes.
  • The solid milk lumps should be strained so that they are completely separated from the milk.
  • After that, enough salt should be added to the lumps of cheese, then they can be left for a few minutes.
  • Store the cheese in round shape in the refrigerator.


Bioteknologi Butter & Buttermilk





Ingredients:

  • Whipped cream
  • Cold water


Instructions:

  • Prepare all the ingredients and equipment. Ensure all necessary items have been gathered.
  • Pour cold water into a large bowl
  • Place a medium bowl on top of a large bowl filled with cold water. This process is done so that the butter can solidify faster.
  • Pour enough whipped cream, stir until separated from the buttermilk
  • If it has been separated, add the buttermilk into a container
  • After that, the butter should be washed with cold water.
  • Then, the butter should be put in a smaller bowl and stirred until combined.
  • The final step is just to transfer the butter to a container, so it can be stored in the refrigerator and can also be used when you want to.
